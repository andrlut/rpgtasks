# Privacy Policy — Perceva

**Last updated: [DATA]**

Perceva is a habits and wellness app that helps you build and observe your identity over time. We take your privacy seriously: we collect only what the app needs to work, we never sell your data, and we do not use advertising or tracking.

This policy explains **what data we collect, why, how long we keep it, and how you can delete it**. It is written to meet the EU/UK **General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)** as well as comparable global privacy expectations.

---

## 1. Who we are (data controller)

Perceva is built and maintained by an independent developer. For anything related to privacy and data protection, contact our **privacy contact**:

- **Email:** privacy@perceva.app

---

## 2. Data we collect

We only collect what you provide by using the app. There is no background collection beyond what's needed to authenticate your account.

### 2.1 Account data
| Data | Description |
|---|---|
| **Email** | Used to create your account, authenticate sign-in, and send magic links / password recovery. |
| **Display name** | An optional name you choose to personalize the app. |
| **Locale** | Your pt-BR or en-US preference, so the app appears in your language. |

### 2.2 Self-knowledge responses and results (wellness data)
The app offers **self-reflection and self-knowledge** instruments. Your answers and the scores computed from them are linked to your account:

- **Big Five (120 items)** — personality inventory;
- **ECR-R (36 items)** — attachment styles in relationships;
- **Schwartz Values (57 items)** — personal values;
- **Wellbeing self-assessment (24 items)** — how you perceive your own life dimensions.

> **Important:** these instruments are **personal wellness and self-knowledge** tools. They are **not** a diagnosis, clinical assessment, treatment, or medical/psychological advice. If you are seeking mental-health care, please consult a qualified professional.

### 2.3 In-app activity
Records generated by your use, all tied to your account:

- Tasks, rewards, skills, and quests you create or adopt;
- Task completions, XP, coins, and streaks (streak/momentum);
- Assessment history (assessment log) and character progression.

### 2.4 Notification preferences
Your reminder times and on/off settings for **local** reminders (e.g. daily brief and checkpoint).

---

## 3. Data we do NOT collect

To be explicit, Perceva does **not** collect or access:

- ❌ Location (GPS or approximate);
- ❌ Contacts, calendar, or friend lists;
- ❌ Camera, photos, or microphone;
- ❌ Biometric data;
- ❌ Data obtained from third parties;
- ❌ Third-party analytics tools;
- ❌ Advertising or ad identifiers;
- ❌ **Tracking** across apps or websites (we do not use App Tracking Transparency because there is no tracking);
- ❌ Remote **push** notifications — all reminders are **local**, scheduled and delivered on your own device (no push server, no push token).

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for commercial or marketing purposes.

---

## 4. Why we use each type of data (purpose and legal basis — GDPR)

| Purpose | Data used | Legal basis (GDPR Art. 6) |
|---|---|---|
| Create and authenticate your account | Email | Performance of a contract (Art. 6(1)(b)) |
| Make the app work (save tasks, progress, results) | Activity, responses/scores, name, locale | Performance of a contract (Art. 6(1)(b)) |
| Show your self-knowledge results | Instrument answers and scores | Consent (Art. 6(1)(a)) — you choose to respond |
| Send reminders you set up | Notification preferences | Consent (Art. 6(1)(a)) |
| Security and abuse prevention | Email, session data | Legitimate interests (Art. 6(1)(f)) |

You can withdraw consent at any time by stopping use of the instruments, turning off notifications, or deleting your account (Section 8).

## 5. Where your data lives (processor / sub-processors)

Perceva uses **Supabase** as its database and authentication provider. Supabase acts as a **data processor** on our behalf and runs on **Amazon Web Services (AWS)** cloud infrastructure.

- **Primary processor:** Supabase Inc.
- **Infrastructure sub-processor:** Amazon Web Services (AWS)
- **Storage region:** data is hosted on AWS servers and may involve an **international transfer** of your data outside your country of residence.

By using the app, you agree to this transfer, which is carried out with appropriate contractual safeguards (e.g. Standard Contractual Clauses where applicable). Database access is protected by **Row-Level Security (RLS)**: each user can only read and write their own data.

---

## 6. How long we keep your data (retention)

We keep your data **for as long as your account exists**. When you delete your account (Section 8), all personal data linked to it is **permanently and irreversibly removed** via cascade (account → character → tasks, rewards, skills, quests, responses, scores, and history). We keep no shadow copies after deletion, except where a specific legal obligation requires it.

---

## 7. Your rights (GDPR)

You have the right to:

- **Access** your personal data;
- **Rectify** inaccurate or outdated data (name and locale are editable in the app);
- **Erase** your data ("right to be forgotten");
- **Restrict** or **object** to certain processing;
- **Data portability**;
- **Withdraw consent** at any time.

To exercise any right not directly available in the app, email **privacy@perceva.app**. We respond within a reasonable time. If you are in the EU/UK, you also have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data-protection authority.

---

## 8. How to delete your account and data

You can erase **everything** directly in the app — no need to contact anyone:

1. Open the app and go to **Settings**;
2. Tap **Delete account**;
3. Confirm.

This triggers deletion of your authentication account and, by cascade, **all** associated personal data (character, tasks, rewards, skills, quests, instrument responses, scores, and history). This action is **permanent and cannot be undone**.

If you prefer, you can also request deletion by email at **privacy@perceva.app**.

---

## 9. Security

- All communication with the server uses **HTTPS/TLS**;
- **Row-Level Security** enforces per-user isolation in the database;
- The app uses only the public (publishable) key — no privileged credentials are embedded;
- Passwords are managed by the authentication provider (Supabase Auth) and are never readable by us.

No system is completely immune to risk. In the event of a relevant security incident, we will take the measures and provide the notifications required by applicable law.

---

## 10. Children

Perceva is **not directed at children**. The recommended minimum age is **13**. We chose 13+ because the app involves **self-reflection and introspective questionnaires** that assume a degree of maturity, and to align with app stores' standard minimum-age practices. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If we learn that an account belongs to someone below this age, we will delete it. Minors should use the app with a parent or guardian's supervision and consent.
